Usability testing with paper prototype


Quick paper prototype was used in early usability testing. This helped us identify and priortize necessary features. This also led us to research more on simplifying the tonal classes.

Restructuring tonality:


User research led us to remove non measurable tones like "joy", "anticipation", and "popularity" from the previous list. Simplified the classes, classified it under 'Persuation' and 'Moments'.

Final product scored users on "Trust", "Confidence", and "Likability", leading to better resonance with the audience.
