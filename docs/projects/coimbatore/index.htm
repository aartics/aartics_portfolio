fun republic mall, coimbatore

As a project architect I designed a new mall in the traditional city of Coimbatore, India

Team size: 3
Duration: 6 months

problem

My challenge as an architect was to design a distinctive new modern mall in the traditional city of Coimbatore

solution

My proposal was to have a unique semi-open mall with a facade that is reminescent of the palm trees and traditional sites that are Coimbatore's identity

process

Sketches    |     Visualizations    |     3D models    |     Construction documents

project context

Role: Architect, Arris Architects, Mumbai

 

Project description:

solution

design concept

I conducted several rapid design iterations with computer generated 3D models, to be able to present several design options. The final design aesthetic is inspired by forms that are reminiscent of the local motifs such as the delicate textiles and the tall palm trees. We also innovated on the planning, by proposing a semi-open air mall for climatic benefit.
