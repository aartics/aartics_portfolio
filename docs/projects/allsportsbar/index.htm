all sports bar, mumbai

As a project architect for 'All Sports Bar', I converted an empty warehouse into a cool new bar in 25 days, and the project is featured in a Bollywood movie!

Team size: 1
Duration: 1 month

problem

As a designer for a bar, my challenge was to convert an empty warehouse into a cool new distinctive sports var in 25 days

solution

I planned a diagonally placed large screen seen my the whole space and street to attract and engage all patrons. The interior space is tied in together with a distinctive bright green band that flows through and becomes furnitures as a spatial band that brands the space.
