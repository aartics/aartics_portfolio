city boulevard, assen

As an urban planner working with Franz Ziegler Studio in The Netherlands, we mapped a detailed proposal for changing the main city corridor of Assen, NL

Team size: 3
Duration: 6 months

problem

The small town of Assen in The Netherlands is looking to densify the town center with the new station. This needs upgrading and revitalizing the main city boulevard also considering minimum changes to existing infrastructure.

solution

Adding strategic new program and projects along the main city corridor, densifying commercial spaces and widening the boulevard while re-ordering private and public transport.

process

Masterplanning    |     Large scaled model making    |     Illustrative communication through plans and renders
