Thesis for M.Sc. Urbanism, T.U. Delft, The Netherlands

3D concept rendering for new waterfront development


Geographically situated 3D model of part of the city of Mumbai, showing the design proposal for corridors connecting the hills to the wetlands with new forms of urbanism, that will harvest water and grow into a new waterfront and bridge connecting across the bay.

Phased out Masterplan for a new "Wetland Economic Zone"
Mapping the urban growth of Munbai


Collected and studied several historic maps and data of Mumbai city to show how the city grew from a few fishing islands to the booming urban center it is now.

Based on documents from the city development authorities, I also mapped the upcoming future plans for the city and presented the possible climatic dangers of this unmanaged growth.

Design Dissertation, M.Sc Urbanism, Netherlands

This project was presented for the final planning and design dissertation project for my Masters in Urbanism from T.U. Delft, as part of the inter-university program - European Masters in Urbanism
