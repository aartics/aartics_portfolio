recentering mumbai

Thesis for M.Sc. Urbanism, T.U. Delft, The Netherlands

Team size: 1
Duration: 6 months

problem

The politician-builder lobby nexus is proposing to reclaim a large portion of Mumbai's ecologically critical wetlands for profit. My urban design dissertation dares to propose alternative futures.

solution

A new form of urbanism in a special "Wetland Economic Zone W.E.Z" in Mumbai which included: 1. Using the topography of the land to harvest water and prevent flooding. 2. Start new forms of productive landscapes with the wetlands instead of destroying them. 3. A new bridge across the bay to connect Mumbai and Navi Mumbai.

process

Research    |     Illustrative mapping    |     Design development using CAD and SketchUP    |     Policy creation
